About me

My name is Andrea Herrera, I was born on April 23rd, 1994 in Caracas,Venezuela.I can say that my passion for animation started at an early age. I can still remeber waking up early every saturday to watch the saturday cartoons. Although, I never thought I would end up doing cartoons for a living, I always had a passion for them. What I love about animation is the ability to create a character and see it come to life little by little as well as seeing the character's personality shape out.
